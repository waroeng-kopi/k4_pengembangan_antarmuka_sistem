About me

Hey there! I'm Adam Rahmat Ilahi, a passionate junior full-stack developer.

With over 5 years of experience in the field, I've had the opportunity to work on various projects and develop a strong foundation in web development. From front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to back-end frameworks such as Node.js, I enjoy tackling challenges and crafting efficient and elegant solutions.

Linux has been my operating system of choice for both personal and professional use. I appreciate the freedom it offers and the robustness it brings to my development environment. As a Linux enthusiast, I love customizing window managers to create visually appealing and functional desktop setups. You might even find my creations showcased on Reddit's r/unixporn, where I share my passion for personalized Linux setups with the community.

Currently, I'm pursuing my studies at the Institute of Technology in Surabaya to expand my knowledge and skills even further. I firmly believe in the importance of lifelong learning, as technology is constantly evolving. I enjoy staying up to date with the latest trends and advancements in the industry, allowing me to bring fresh perspectives to my work.
